How to make Shakshuka

Description

This smoothie is good, easy to make, and very customizable. This is just the way I make it, but feel free to change it to your tastes or add new ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Strawberries, preferrably frozen
  • Blueberries, preferrably frozen
  • Honey
  • Greek Yogurt of your choice
  • Water or whatever milk you like

Steps

  1. Add the ingredients into a blender
  2. Blend until the smoothie is your preferred consistency
