How to make Shakshuka

Description

Shakshuka doesn't have a lot of ingredients (but you can add more) and it's easy to make. It's kind of like mom's huevos rancheros but with a lot more emphasis on tomatoes. Follow these steps and you'll have a delicious meal for any time of the day.

Ingredients

  • 2 Tbsp Olive Oil
  • 1 diced onion
  • 1/2 cup of mushrooms
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 diced bell pepper
  • 1 seeded and sliced jalapeno or serrano
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1 can of crushed tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup of water
  • 4 eggs
  • Crumbled feta cheese or queso fresco
  • Chopped cilantro

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a large pan over medium-high heat. Add onions and mushrooms and season with salt. Cook until mushrooms start to brown, approx. 10 minutes
  2. Add bell peppers and jalapeno. Cook and stir until peppers begin to soften up, approx. 5 minutes
  3. Add the seasonings and stir for about a minute
  4. Add crushed tomatoes and a bit of water if the tomatoes arent liquidy enough. Lower heat to medium and simmer uncovered, stirring occasionally for 15-20 minues. Add water if sauce if too thik for your liking.
  5. Once sauce has reduced and is thick, make little divets in the sauce for you to put your eggs in. Cover pan and cook until eggs are to your desired doneness.
  6. Remove from heat and add chopped cilantro and your choice of cheese before serving.
